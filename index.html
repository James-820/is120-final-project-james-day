Weightlift Tracking

How To Use

To add a new lift, click the "Add Lift" button, fill out the information, and click "Submit". Click "Cancel Add" to cancel.

The "Reps" is simply a text input, so feel free to add anything you find helpful. For example, you might add "8, 7, Failure" to mean 8 reps on the first set, 7 reps on the second set, and going until failure on the third, or "6, 6" to mean two sets of 6 reps each.

To edit an existing lift, click the lift's "Edit" button. Simply edit the current information and hit "Save Changes" to confirm.

Stay safe, and happy lifting!
